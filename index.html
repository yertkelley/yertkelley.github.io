About the Firm

A Different Kind
of Capital Partner

Founded on the principle that the best investment is ownership in a business you understand deeply, Ark Squared Capital focuses on concentrated, long-duration positions in companies with durable competitive advantages.

We are not driven by quarterly benchmarks or fund cycles. Our capital is permanent. Our perspective is generational. Our commitment to our partners is unconditional.

We look for businesses with strong free cash flow generation, honest and capable management, and pricing power that compounds quietly over decades.

Investment Philosophy

Three Pillars of
Permanent Capital

01

Quality Over Quantity

We hold concentrated positions in a small number of exceptional businesses rather than diversifying across mediocrity. Understanding each holding completely is a prerequisite, not an aspiration.

02

Patience as Strategy

Time is the ally of a wonderful business and the enemy of a poor one. We are willing to sit still when markets are irrational, and act decisively when they present opportunity.

03

Integrity as Infrastructure

Every relationship — with portfolio companies, limited partners, and counterparties — is governed by the same standard: honesty, transparency, and long-term alignment of interest.
